VISA AND ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

All holiday and business travellers applying for a visa to Sri Lanka must have an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) to enter the island. For more information visit http://www.eta.gov.lk


1. Tourist Visa

This visa is eligible for tourists who wish to enter Sri Lanka for sightseeing, relaxation, excursions, visiting relatives, etc. for a short duration


2. Business Visa

This visa is eligible for foreign nationals who wish to enter Sri Lanka for business purposes, for a short duration. Applications for group visas are also provided at www.eta.gov.lk.
